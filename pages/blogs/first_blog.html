Why Did I Make this Website

8/15/25 6:02PM

So at the end of the spring 2025 semester, I had a little bug in my brain saying "make a website, it will be so fun and cool". I had an internship during the summer (more on that in another post at some point), so I didn't really have the time to start on it during then. Towards the end of the internship I was just browsing epik.com and I saw "wyattborden.com" for only $15.99 a year. When I saw that, I bought it on impulse. I had to make sure that no one would have it. After that I had to learn HTML and CSS and then here we are. Hopefully, you will be able to enjoy this space, and I will not forget to update it. There is a high possibility that this website will just rot.

Some of the things that I hope to have on here are:

  • About me page
  • Projects page
  • Blogs
  • Other things that I can't think of at the moment

In a pefect world, I want this place will work as an extension of my resume. A place were I can have more information about myself that recruiters can look at to learn more about me.

Anyways, I will get on with making the website so there is something to look at.
